Top Places to Visit in Ethiopia

Lalibela

Distance from Addis Ababa: 645 km

Flight Duration: 1 hour 15 minutes

Estimated Flight Cost: $100 - $150

Famous for its rock-hewn churches, Lalibela is one of Ethiopia’s most important historical sites.

Axum

Distance from Addis Ababa: 988 km

Flight Duration: 1 hour 45 minutes

Estimated Flight Cost: $120 - $180

Known for its ancient obelisks and the legendary Queen of Sheba’s palace.

Simien Mountains

Distance from Addis Ababa: 750 km

Flight Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Estimated Flight Cost: $110 - $160

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning landscapes and wildlife.
